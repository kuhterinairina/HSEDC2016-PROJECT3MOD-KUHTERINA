VAN GOGH

ABOUT
ARTIST

Dutch Post-Impressionist painter who is among
the most famous and influential figures in the
history of Western art. In just over a decade he
created about 2,100 artworks, including around
860 oil paintings, most of them in the last two
years of his life in France, where he died.
